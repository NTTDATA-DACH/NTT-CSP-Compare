2. Cost Efficiency & TCO

Scaleway's pricing model offers a unique arbitrage opportunity. While the Kosmos control plane is premium-priced (~€105/mo), it allows us to attach ultra-low-cost external nodes (e.g., commodity bare metal) to the cluster. This significantly lowers the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for compute-heavy workloads compared to STACKIT, where the control plane (~€72/mo) is paired with a more restricted ecosystem.

3. Recommendation

  • Choose Scaleway Kosmos for 90% of use cases: hybrid cloud orchestration, avoiding vendor lock-in, and optimizing compute costs via external nodes.
  • Choose STACKIT Edge Cloud only if the requirement is strictly for disconnected industrial operations within a German sovereign regulatory framework.
