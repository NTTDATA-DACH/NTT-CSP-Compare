Overarching Summary

The strategic landscape between Azure and Scaleway represents a definitive choice between a comprehensive Enterprise Control Plane and a High-Efficiency Infrastructure Utility. Azure is the mandatory choice for governance, identity (Entra ID), global networking, and proprietary intelligence (OpenAI, T-SQL); it is the 'brain' of the architecture, offering unparalleled depth in compliance and integrated tooling but at a significant financial premium involving complex metering. Scaleway, conversely, acts as the 'muscle,' offering raw compute, storage, and bandwidth at 50-90% lower costs with a predictable flat-rate model.
