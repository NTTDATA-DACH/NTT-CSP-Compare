Overarching Summary

The 2026 strategic comparison between Azure and Scaleway reveals a definitive split between 'Enterprise Platform' and 'Utility Infrastructure.' Azure remains the superior choice for mission-critical, regulated, and complex global architectures, driven by its dominance in Identity (Entra ID), proprietary AI access (GPT-5/o1), and comprehensive governance tools.
