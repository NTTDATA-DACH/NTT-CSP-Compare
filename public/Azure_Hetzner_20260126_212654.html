Overarching Summary

The strategic imperative for our infrastructure is a hybrid 'Best-of-Breed' architecture rather than a single-vendor commitment. Azure serves as the 'Enterprise Nervous System,' providing the necessary governance, global scale, and PaaS capabilities for core production applications, complex orchestration, and regulated data. However, its consumption-based pricing model is punitively expensive for high-bandwidth and raw storage workloads. Hetzner acts as the 'Infrastructure Engine,' offering unbeatable price-to-performance for bulk storage, heavy compute, and egress-heavy tasks without the 'cloud tax.' We recommend authorizing Azure for agility, security, and compliance-bound workloads, while aggressively offloading static, resource-intensive, and bandwidth-heavy operations to Hetzner to optimize operating margins.

We will leverage Azure for innovation and elasticity, and Hetzner for sustained, heavy lifting.

Monitoring

Therefore, Azure must be the standard for all business intelligence and telemetry, with Hetzner tools used solely for redundant connectivity alerts.

Security and Governance

Azure delivers premium, programmable security layers with strict SLAs and forensic capabilities, acting as necessary 'insurance' for mission-critical assets and compliance-heavy environments. Hetzner provides robust, zero-cost, 'always-on' perimeter defense that is sufficient for general infrastructure hygiene but lacks the visibility, audit trails, and customizable logic required by regulated industries. We will invest in Azure for targeted threat protection while relying on Hetzner's baseline for commodity resources.
