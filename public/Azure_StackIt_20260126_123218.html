While the Free and Basic tiers ($74/mo) are excellent for prototyping, production SLAs require tripling resources, pushing costs above $700/mo for moderate datasets. STACKIT offers a massive value advantage for production, including 3-node HA in its base pricing. For a standard HA cluster, STACKIT is approximately 75% cheaper than Azure.

Final Verdict

  • Choose Azure AI Search if your primary KPI is relevance quality for GenAI apps and you need to minimize engineering time.
  • Choose STACKIT OpenSearch for log analytics, high-volume keyword search, or if your budget demands cost-effective High Availability.
